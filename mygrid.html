Our Mission

Our Mission is to guide refugees and immigrants in the journey towards self-determination and social equality by providing and connecting them to available programs/services that will improve their healthcare, education as well as their economic well-being.We have been Serving Immigrants, refugees and people in Need Since 2015. Here are Our Values , and we believe that the best Way to serve God is to serve people,”.

Training & Educational Programs

Why? Because the community we serve is mostly Africans and we understand African-cultures and believes. We serve the immigrant communities of Brooklyn Park, Brooklyn Center and the entire north western part of Hennepin County. How Do We Work? By partnering with other organizations in the community that have a long, proven history of helping refugees (like CAPI, Planned Parenthood, City of Brooklyn Park, Minnesota Workforce Center, WAFCS and GFF) we work smarter and faster and with great humility in everything we do.
